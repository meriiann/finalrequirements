Jewel of the Sugar Island

Short Background

Sipalay City is known for "THE JEWEL OF THE SUGAR ISLAND". It comes from a very rich history and background of the City of Sipalay famous for the minerals beneath the natural beauty of the landscapes that cover the entire city.

The town is located in the south of Negros, a province known as the center of the sugar industry in the philippines, that's why its called Jewel of the Sugar Island. One of its many beaches is even named "Sugar Beach"

Why Choose Sipalay City

Sipalay City is a coastal paradise! come and enjoy breathtaking views and endless adventures. You can enjoy a beautiful view of sunsets and sunrise. An instagrammable views, where you can relax by the rhythmic sound of waves.

What are you waiting for? pack your swimsuits, sunscreen and an open mind and get ready to create memories that will last a lifetime. Come and discover why our city is famous when we talk about beaches and resorts.
